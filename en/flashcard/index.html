Set up Flash card study plan

Begin your study by narrow down you will study through Subjects(required), Exam (CE/AL/DSE), Language, Year, Paper, Section, Tags (Topic), etc.

Also, the amount of card you study per day can be changed ( default is 20 per day).

Each subject can have ONE(1) Study Plan at a time, while Progress (marking a question hard or easy) is stored per unique question and independent from the Plan.

Changing Study Plan will not clean Progress, i.e. Cards/Questions you marked as easy will still appear less often.

(No need to pick all filters, those unchange will be ignored. Since not every combination has results, picking too many filters may found zero related question.)

e.g.

  1. You must select a subject, options related to that subject will be loaded afterward.

  2. Then, you can focus on Tags: Acid and Bases and study all questions reguardless of paper language, year, etc.

  3. Alternative to 2., you can focus on the year 2020, just like having a mock exam.

Playing flashcards are not the only way to use this site, you are free to browse any questions or search for the related answers.
