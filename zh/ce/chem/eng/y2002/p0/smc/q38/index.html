CE_02_38
Ans: B
Set-ups (1) and (3) can be used to dry moist sulphur dioxide gas.



  1. Anhydrous calcium choride is neutral and has no reaction with sulphur dioxide gas.

  2. Calcium oxide is a base and it will react with SO2 which is acidic. Hence cannot be used as a dryer.
    3.Concentrated sulphuric acid is acidic and has no reaction with SO2, Concentrated acid has drying effect (they long for water to dilute themselves).
